Our Story

From Jollibee to Forever

First Meet & Date

Nov 2016

Harvy and Christine first met in November 2016 at Jollibee in Gaisano Mactan. After many attempts to plan a meet-up, the day finally came when both of them were finally available. Christine was the one who initiated the plan, while Harvy arrived feeling both excited and very nervous.

Christine came straight from school that day, carrying a big shoulder bag. One of the most memorable moments from their first meeting was when Harvy jokingly asked if she was planning to move in already since it looked like she had packed all her things in that bag. The joke made them both laugh and instantly broke the ice.

Despite enjoying their time together, Harvy kept asking Christine if they would ever meet again. He was worried that their first meeting might also be their last, as he was nervous and a little afraid that the moment might not happen again.

Fortunately, that day was not the end—it was just the beginning. What started as a simple meet-up turned into a beautiful journey filled with laughter, adventures, and a shared love for traveling. Today, they look forward to continuing that journey together as they begin their new chapter as husband and wife.

She said 'Yes'

14 Feb 2017

Harvy had been courting Christine since November 2016, patiently showing his love and making her feel special. After months of small gestures, laughter, and shared moments, the day finally came when Christine said yes to starting their relationship.

It happened on Valentine’s Day, February 14, 2017, at USJR campus—the very place where their story began and where they first caught glimpses of each other. To make the moment unforgettable, Harvy gave her 101 reasons why he loved her, carefully written and placed inside a jar.

That simple yet meaningful gesture marked the beginning of a beautiful journey together. From that day on, their bond grew stronger, filled with laughter, love, and countless adventures—leading them to the wedding day they now share with family and friends.

The Proposal

09 NOV 2024

Harvy had been dreaming of the perfect moment to ask Christine to marry him. The plan was set for Boracay Island, Philippines, and he enlisted the help of Christine’s sister to ask for their parents’ blessing and make sure everything went smoothly.

The trip was presented as a simple family getaway, but in truth, it was carefully planned for a proposal. Harvy insisted on bringing both families along, using it as the perfect alibi for what was about to happen.

The magic unfolded at Puka Beach, with the sun shining down on the white sand and the gentle waves around them. Surrounded by their families and the people enjoying the beach, Harvy asked Christine to spend the rest of her life with him. It was a moment full of love, laughter, and unforgettable memories—the beginning of a new chapter in their story together.
